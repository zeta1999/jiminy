21 class JiminyCartPoleEnv(RobotJiminyEnv):
22  """
23  @brief Implementation of a Gym environment for the Cartpole which is using
24  Jiminy Engine to perform physics computations and Gepetto-viewer for
25  rendering. It is a specialization of RobotJiminyGoalEnv. The Cartpole
26  is a pole attached by an un-actuated joint to a cart. The goal is to
27  prevent the pendulum from falling over by increasing and reducing the
28  cart's velocity.
29 
30  @details **OBSERVATION:**
31  Type: Box(4)
32  Num Observation Min Max
33  0 Cart Position -1.5 1.5
34  1 Cart Velocity -Inf Inf
35  2 Pole Angle -50 deg 50 deg
36  3 Pole Velocity At Tip -Inf Inf
37 
38  **ACTIONS:**
39  Type: Discrete(2)
40  Num Action
41  0 Push cart to the left
42  1 Push cart to the right
43 
44  Note that the amount the velocity that is reduced or increased is not
45  fixed, it depends on the angle the pole is pointing. This is because the
46  center of gravity of the pole increases the amount of energy needed to
47  move the cart underneath it.
48 
49  **REWARD:**
50  Reward is 1 for every step taken, including the termination step.
51  move the cart underneath it.
52 
53  **STARTING STATE:**
54  All observations are assigned a uniform random value in [-0.05..0.05]
55 
56  **EPISODE TERMINATION:**
57  If any of these conditions is satisfied:
58  - Pole Angle is more than 25 degrees Cart
59  - Position is more than 75 cm
60  - Episode length is greater than 200
61 
62  **SOLVED REQUIREMENTS:**
63  Considered solved when the average reward is greater than or equal to
64  195.0 over 100 consecutive trials.
65  """
